Introduction

Tufail bin Amr

Abu Huraira

Al Sufa

The Hunger of Abu Huraira

  • Abu Huraira tied stones to his stomach because he was starving
  • One time Abu Bakr passed by he asked a question about the Quran
  • Abu Huraira already knew the answer but wanted to be repaid by food
  • Abu Bakr answered the question and moved on
  • Then Umar passed by and was asked the question, he answered and moved on
  • Then Prophet came and the Prophet smiled at him
  • The Prophet brought Abu Huraira and he said to call all the people of Sufa
  • Abu Huraira is passing the milk to every person of Sufa
  • The Prophet then told Abu Huraira to drink the milk
  • Abu Huraira drank until he was full then the Prophet drank the rest of the milk

Abu Huraira Meets the Devil

  • Abu Huraira was asked to guard something by the Prophet
  • An old man came to steal something
  • Abu Huraira warned him that he will report him to the prophet
  • The old man begged him not to and promised he will not steal from him again
  • Then the Prophet told Abu Huraira what did your prisoner do last night
  • Abu Huraira mentioned the incident and The Prophet told him that he lied to you and he will return
  • Abu Huraira grabbed the thieve and says I will take you to the Prophet
  • The next day the Prophet said what did your prisoner do last night
  • Abu Huraira said he was begging me and I felt sorry for him
  • The Prophet said he is lying to you and he will return again
  • Abu Huraira met him a third time and he said if you leave me I will teach you some words
  • He said say Ayat ul Kursi before you sleep every night
  • Abu Huraira let him go
  • The Prophet said what did you prisoner do last night and Abu Huraira told him
  • The Prophet said he spoke the truth but he is still a liar and that was the shaytan
