Maymuna Bint Haarith

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

Introduction

  • Maymuna's name was Bara and it was changed by the prophet to Maymuna
  • The Prophet wanted to establish ties with tribe of Zainab Bint Harith
  • Zainab Bint Haarith is the half sister of Maymuna
  • Maymuna is from the first to accept Islam
  • Maymuna is a brave women and accepted Islam early on
  • Maymuna was married to Masud bin Amr in the days of ignorance than they divorced
  • Maymuna then married Abu ruh then he died
  • Maymuna did not have any children
  • Al Abbas was in charge of Maymuna who was married to her sister

The Marriage Between Maymuna and Muhammad

  • Maymuna proposes to the Prophet and asked Al Abbas to apporach the Prophet
  • Surah Al Ahzab verse 50 was revealed
  • يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّبِىُّ إِنَّآ أَحْلَلْنَا لَكَ أَزْوَٰجَكَ ٱلَّـٰتِىٓ ءَاتَيْتَ أُجُورَهُنَّ وَمَا مَلَكَتْ يَمِينُكَ مِمَّآ أَفَآءَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيْكَ وَبَنَاتِ عَمِّكَ وَبَنَاتِ عَمَّـٰتِكَ وَبَنَاتِ خَالِكَ وَبَنَاتِ خَـٰلَـٰتِكَ ٱلَّـٰتِى هَاجَرْنَ مَعَكَ وَٱمْرَأَةًۭ مُّؤْمِنَةً إِن وَهَبَتْ نَفْسَهَا لِلنَّبِىِّ إِنْ أَرَادَ ٱلنَّبِىُّ أَن يَسْتَنكِحَهَا خَالِصَةًۭ لَّكَ مِن دُونِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ ۗ قَدْ عَلِمْنَا مَا فَرَضْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ فِىٓ أَزْوَٰجِهِمْ وَمَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَـٰنُهُمْ لِكَيْلَا يَكُونَ عَلَيْكَ حَرَجٌۭ ۗ وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ غَفُورًۭا رَّحِيمًۭا
  • O Prophet! We have made lawful for you your wives to whom you have paid their ˹full˺ dowries as well as those ˹bondwomen˺ in your possession, whom Allah has granted you.1 And ˹you are allowed to marry˺ the daughters of your paternal uncles and aunts, and the daughters of your maternal uncles and aunts, who have emigrated like you. Also ˹allowed for marriage is˺ a believing woman who offers herself to the Prophet ˹without dowry˺ if he is interested in marrying her—˹this is˺ exclusively for you, not for the rest of the believers.2 We know well what ˹rulings˺ We have ordained for the believers in relation to their wives and those ˹bondwomen˺ in their possession.   • maymuna is a Believing women who offers to be in service of Prophet
  • Maymuna wanted the honor and distinction of being mothers of believers
  • The Prophet could not say no to Abbas when he proposed on behalf of Maymuna
  • They got married in the 7th year hijra
  • The Prophet was thinking of peace between two tribes by marrying maymuna
  • When the news reached Maymuna she was on her camel and gave it all for messenger
  • Maymuna means Good fortune and someone who is blessed
  • The Prophet is coming back to Makkah and he marries Maymuna
  • Maymuna had 8 sisters and all of them were married to leaders of tribes
  • All the other tribes accepted Islam as well

The Characteristics of Maymuna

  • From Maymuna we get the Hadith of Ibn Abbas who wanted to spend night with the Prophet
  • Ibn Abbas is the nephew of Maymuna
  • Ibn Abbas said they slept vertically and he slept horizontal
  • From this we get the Hadith of Qiyam ul layl
  • Maymuna was in a lot of debt and Prophet gave her a dua for freeing debt
  • Maymuna's will was that she is buried where she got the news of proposal
  • Maymuna died in 61 hijra
  • Ibn Abbas was very grieved and he said do not shake it or rattle it
  • Maymuna narrated 76 Hadith 1 in Bukhari and 75 in Muslim
  • Ayesha said when Maymuna passed away she had the most taqwa
  • Maymuna was the best in upholding the ties of kinship
