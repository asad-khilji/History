The Five Abdullahs: Introduction

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

Who are the Five Abdullahs?

  1. Abdullah Bin Abbas
  2. Abdullah Bin Umar
  3. Abdullah Bin Amr Al As
  4. Abdullah Bin Zubair
  5. Abdullah Bin Masood

Introduction

  • There are a lot more men named Abdullah then these five
  • It is the most common name
  • This name is most beloved to Allah
  • The best Abdullah is Abu Bakr As Siddiq
  • These five were born in the lifetime of the Prophet
  • They lived long after Prophet
  • They narrated many Hadith
  • They went through a lot of Trials and Tribulations
  • They played a crucial role in the trials
  • They influenced schools of fiqh and hadith narrations
  • They influenced tafseer study
  • Ibn Abbas has the most extensive biography
  • Ibn Abbas had the most special role
