Zainab Bint Khuzaimah

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

The Prophet had two wives named Zainab

This Zainab married Prophet right after Hafsa

The Name Zainab means a beautiful flower

The precious daughter and jewel of her father

Zainab is from a Najdi tribe

Zainab was married to the cousin of the Prophet

Zainab's First Husband was Zaid

Zainab's Second Husband was Abdullah bin Jahsh

Zainab's name was mother of the poor

Zainab did not have any children

Zainab's mother was Hind Bint Oath

Hind Bint Oath married many men which she had lost

Maymunah Bint Harith is sister of Zainab Bint Khuzaimah

Salamah Bint Rumais is sister of Zainab and wife of Hamza

Zainab is the Brother of Khalid bin Waleed

She died in Ramadan 4th year hijra

She died 3 or 4 months after marrying Prophet

The Prophet married her to make peace with the tribe
